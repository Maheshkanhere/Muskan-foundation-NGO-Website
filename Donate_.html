Giving is the Greatest act of Grace !

Being honest and transparent are basic aspects of human nature and we strive to be truthful with you all to build solid friendships, an excellent reputation and a better future for our students and community. All our projects are based on two pillars; our donors and volunteers.

We simply cannot do what we are doing without your support. We are 100% transparent, open and honest about our financials and share information about funding annually. From our donors and supporters to volunteers and workers, we are clear about where their contributions are being used.

Our Financial Report is published annually. The report shows exactly where your investment is being funded. We plan all our projects and once funding is in place we begin to build a better future.
